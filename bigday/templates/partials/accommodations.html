Where to Stay

Below is a brief list of recommendations on places to stay for your visit to Chicago. It is summer-time Chi - a big deal! - so we do recommend reserving your accommodation early. Kelsey and Dain will be staying at their house in West Town, which is about 15 minutes from the venue and hotels. We have reserved a block of rooms at both the JW Marriott and the Residence Inn. Unbooked rooms will be released 30 days prior to the event, so book yours ASAP.

We will provide shuttle service to/from the hotels.

The JW Marriott The The JW Marriott is conveniently located in the loop and very close to the wedding venue. It is about 3 miles from the Planetarium.
Residence Inn Residence Inn Downtown/Loop is the cheaper of the two hotel options. In the heart of the Loop, the Residence Inn is about 3 miles from the Planetarium and just a few blocks from the Mag Mile and the JW Marriott.
Vacation rentals Both VRBO and airbnb have a number of rentals in the area. This is often more affordable option for those who don't mind something a bit more self-service.
